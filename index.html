Whale Evolution

The ancestors and adaptations of the whale over time

Ancestors

Sinonyx

Wolf-like large carnivorous mammal

Existed around 60 million years ago

Indohyus

Semiaquatic racoon-like mammal

Existed around 50 million years ago

Ambulocetus

Fully aquatic mammal with short limbs and large feet for swimming

Existed around 45 million years ago.

Rodhocetus

Short limbs with hands and feet that were probably webbed

Existed around 40 million years ago.

Basilosaurus

Fore limbs more like flippers, hind limbs almost totally gone, and a very long tail for swimming

Existed around 35 million years ago.

Dorudon

Nostrils were midway from snout to top of the head

Existed around 30 million years ago.

Adaptations

Streamlined Body

The body has become more streamlined for swimming

Front Flippers

The forelimbs have modified into flippers

Tail

The tail has broadened horizontally

No Hind Limbs

The hindlimbs have disappeared completely

Blubber

There is now blubber instead of hair

Nostrils

The nostrils are now on the top of head

Relatives

Dolphins

Narwals

Hippos

Environment

Fresh Water Habitat

Bony well developed around the middle ear

Brackish Water Habitat

Developed long powerful tail

Leg shortened

Fat pad in jaw for hearing

Salt Water Habitat

Nostrils shifted back

Eyes moved to side of head

Hind legs completely gone
